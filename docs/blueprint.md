# **App Name**: Patriarcas Revelados

## Core Features:

- Interactive Chapter Exploration: Allows users to explore each chapter of Patriarcas y Profetas with in-depth explanations and historical context.
- Personalized Study Tools: Enables users to annotate, highlight, and bookmark verses for a personalized study experience, syncing across devices.
- Community Discussion Forum: Provides a platform for users to discuss insights, share revelations, and connect with a community of fellow students.
- AI-Powered Insight Tool: Utilizes generative AI to provide additional insights and interpretations of biblical text based on user queries. This AI insight tool decides if it should offer historical, cultural, or spiritual context.
- Audiobook Integration: Includes a complete audiobook version of Patriarcas y Profetas, allowing users to listen while they study or on the go.
- Offline Access: Allows users to access downloaded content, including chapters and audiobooks, even without an internet connection.
- Progress Tracking: Monitors user's progress through the study material, providing visual indicators and milestones to encourage continued engagement.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep gold (#D4AF37) to evoke a sense of history and spirituality.
- Background color: Dark green (#1A3622) to provide a rich and immersive feel.
- Accent color: Soft white (#F5F5DC) for highlights and text to ensure readability.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', serif, for an elegant, fashionable feel.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif, paired with Playfair for readability.
- Use detailed, illustrative icons representing key figures and events from Patriarcas y Profetas.
- Mobile-first design with a clean layout, ensuring optimal viewing and interaction on all devices.
- Subtle animations, like fading in content upon scrolling, to enhance user engagement and create a dynamic experience.
